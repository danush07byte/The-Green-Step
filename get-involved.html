Get Involved

Take action today and help create real change for our planet. Whether you volunteer or join community events, every step you take contributes to a greener and more sustainable future.

Become a Volunteer

Join The Green Step's volunteer community and be part of hands-on climate activities such as clean-ups, tree planting, workshops, and awareness campaigns. Your time and effort can make a meaningful environmental impact.

How It Works

1. Sign Up

Fill in your basic details and let us know your areas of interest.

2. Attend Orientation

Join a short briefing session, online or in-person to understand our programs and volunteer roles.

3. Choose Your Path

Select the type of volunteer work you want to participate in, including clean-ups, event support, or community outreach.

4. Start Volunteering

Begin joining our activities and become part of our growing volunteer community working together for climate action.
