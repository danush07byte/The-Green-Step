Make a Difference With Your Contribution

Your donation helps support climate action programs across Malaysia, from tree planting and waste reduction projects to community education and environmental clean-ups. Every contribution, no matter the amount, brings us one step closer to a greener future.

Why Your Donation Matters

Climate change is affecting Malaysia through rising temperatures, stronger floods, and increasing pollution.

Your support helps us:

  • • Organize environmental clean-ups across beaches, rivers, and parks
  • • Conduct tree-planting campaigns to restore green areas
  • • Educate communities about sustainable living
  • • Support youth climate awareness programs
  • • Fund materials, tools, and volunteer resources

Together, we can create cleaner, safer environments for Malaysian communities.
